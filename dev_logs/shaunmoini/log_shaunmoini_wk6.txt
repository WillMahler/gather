- Implemented user authentication with Firebase
	- Users can sign in using their existing Google account
	- Users can also register with email
- Implemented 'forgot password' function that enables users to reset their password
- Also put in place email verification method that requires users to supply a legitimate email during registration
- Once a user is signed in they can not go back to log in screen unless they log out from profile page
- Once a user is signed in, they will remain signed in even if app is closed and re-launched
